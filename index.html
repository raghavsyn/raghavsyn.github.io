Who we are?

A management + technology consulting firm, holistic is the operative word!

 

We help early stage enterprises going from 0-to-1 become future-proof, agile, sustainable and scalable.

What are we trying to solve?

Creating a product (or a service) is hard. It is doubly hard when one has to build and scale a company from scratch. We want to make the process a touch easier.

 

Founders have to take multiple high-stakes decisions, all-day, everyday. From picking a tech stack to picking your first team. From picking the correct database to spending on the right office space.

 

We help these startups take more informed decisions by presenting the most contextual & optimal solutions. Once these decisions are made, we implement them too!
